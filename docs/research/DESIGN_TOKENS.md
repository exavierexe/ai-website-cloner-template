# Grease Monkey Design Tokens

## Colors
- **Brand Primary (Orange):** `#ff4d24` (used for buttons, accents, icons)
- **Background (Dark):** `#000000` or very dark grey (`#111111`) for the site body.
- **Text:** White (`#ffffff`) for main body text, and some light grey for secondary text.
- **Accent/Hover:** `#e64420` (darker orange for hover states)
- **Borders/Dividers:** Dark grey (e.g., `#333333`)

## Typography
- **Headings (h1-h6):** Bold sans-serif or a strong industrial font (we'll use 'Geist' or 'Inter' with bold weights to match the "Grease Monkey" brand). In the original it seems they used a standard font, but their logo implies a strong, industrial look. The template has `Domaine` serif which we should remove and use a strong sans-serif.
- **Body:** Standard sans-serif (`Geist` or system-ui).

## Spacing & Layout
- **Grid:** Container with max-width (around 1200px - 1440px).
- **Gaps:** Generous spacing between sections (e.g., `py-20` or `py-24`).
- **Product Cards:** Clean images with dark backgrounds, orange price tags, and orange "Add to cart" buttons.

## Components to Build
1. **Header:** Sticky, dark background, centered or left-aligned logo, right-aligned cart/menu.
2. **Hero:** Full width background image with strong orange typography.
3. **Product Slider/Grid:** 3 or 4 column grid for products.
4. **Testimonial Slider:** Dark background, white text.
5. **Footer:** Multi-column layout with social icons and newsletter signup.
